Benas Bitvinskas

Founder of helloGap

What kind of monk is he?

Benas is the author of helloGap, which is now available worldwide. He believes that this project will help people to understand the whole meditation purpose. By following some meditation experts he decided to make something everyone could taste and use daily. He made a simple 10 minute session which includes Andy's voice and natural ocean + dolphin background noise.

Currently Benas is spending his days in school. Learning about the purpose of life and thinking what can make him a better person. As you meditate, he says, you can see where your attention is naturally drawn and you focus what you can fix. Once you fight with your mind you need to gently bring it back to the experience of nature and live with the silence.

Why hellogap?

Benas is always going through the meditation journey and this project actually helps him to concentrate and to explain how easy it is to give your mind 10 minutes a day to relax.
